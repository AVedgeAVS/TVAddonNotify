New update for AVEdge SE. ***v18.4.12, updated 12-Apr-2018***
Great news loyal AVEdge SE users, we've now transitioned you from 
the SE to the HUB. The AVEdge HUB is our most feature filled build 
for AVEdge. You now have all the latest plugins and our exciting HUB 
user interface for your streaming pleasure. There's lots of new things 
to discover, so start using the HUB right away. Enjoy!

To download this latest version: Go to the App draw and 
click on the AVEdge Updater SE app. Click 'I Agree' and wait for 
the new version to download and install. 
Allow any add-on updates to complete before streaming.
