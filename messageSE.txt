New update for AVEdge HUB SE. ***v8.1.0, updated 21-Nov-2017***

Changed backgrounds, added a new section 'All in One', changed submenu
for LIVE TV. Removed dead add-ons and fixed some minor bugs.

To check your current version; inside AVEdge SE, click on SYSTEM, 
then scroll down to SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME. To get 
the latest update: First quit out of AVEdge SE, then open the App 
draw and click on AVEdge Updater SE. Click 'I Agree' and wait for 
the new version to download and install. Allow any Add-on updates 
to complete before streaming.
