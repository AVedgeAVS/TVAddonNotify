New update for AVEdge SE. ***v8.0.3, updated 16-Oct-2017***

Fixed some minor bugs and added RSS feeds.

To check your current version; inside AVEdge SE, click on SYSTEM, then 
scroll down to SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME. To get the latest 
update: First quit out of AVEdge SE, then open the App draw and click 
on AVEdge Updater SE. Click 'I Agree' and wait for the new version 
to download and install. Allow any Add-on updates to complete before 
streaming.
