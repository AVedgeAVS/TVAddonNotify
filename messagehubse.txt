New update for AVEdge HUB SE. ***v8.0.4, updated 06-Nov-2017***

Fixed some minor bugs with the launcher screen.

To check your current version; inside AVEdge SE, click on SYSTEM, 
then scroll down to SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME. To get 
the latest update: First quit out of AVEdge HUB SE, then open the App 
draw and click on AVEdge HUB. Click on the BUILD icon, then click on
the AVEdge SE icon. Click 'Install' to install the new version. Allow 
any Add-on updates to complete before streaming.
