New update for AVEdge HUB SE. ***v8.2.1, updated 16-Jan-2018***

Added more live TV add-ons, UK TV channels, Eastern European, and 
Spanish. Made menus more readable, fixed some IPTV.

To download the latest update: Open AVEdge HUB, click on the BUILD 
tile, then click on the AVEdge SE icon. Click 'Install', then click 
'I Agree'. This will download and install the lastest version of 
the build. Now click 'Yes' to launch the build. Allow any add-on 
updates to complete before streaming.
