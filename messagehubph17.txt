New update for AVEdge HUB build. ***v17.11.23, updated 23-Nov-2017***

Changed LIVE TV submenu. Added new add-ons for movies, TV shows and 
live TV. Removed dead add-ons and fixed some minor bugs.

To check your current version; inside AVEdge SE, click on SYSTEM, 
then scroll down to SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME. To get 
the latest update: First quit out of AVEdge HUB, then open the App 
draw and click on AVEdge HUB. Click on the BUILD icon, then click on
the AVEdge HUB icon. Click 'Install' to install the new version. 
Allow any Add-on updates to complete before streaming.
