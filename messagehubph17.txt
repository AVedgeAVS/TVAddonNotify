New update for AVEdge HUB build. ***v17.12.4, updated 4-Dec-2017***

Added a password to the Adult menu. Added movie a TV show apps to 
Apps menu.

Check your current version by clicking on SYSTEM. The version number is  
in the lower right corner of the screen. To get the latest update 
of the AVEdge HUB build, open AVEdge HUB app and click on the BUILD 
icon. Choose the AVEdge HUB icon. Click 'Install', then click 
'I Agree' to download the new version. Now click 'Yes' to launch the 
build. Allow any add-on updates to complete before streaming.
