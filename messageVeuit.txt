Welcome to Veu-It, a simple media center app that lets you stream 
your favourite movies, TV shows, music, and even find live TV 
channels. There are many ways for you to stream, what you want, in HD 
with no buffering or freezing. Just click on the menu section and 
find what you are looking for.

Veu-It has an Update feature in the SETTINGS menu that you can run 
at any time to update or reinstall the app. Remember you must 
'Force Stop' Veu-It after running the update so that it can install 
the new app properly. 

We hope you enjoy Veu-It.
