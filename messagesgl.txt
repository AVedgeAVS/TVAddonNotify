Hello from us at AVEdge. With all the changes to the plugins that work in the system, 
we've worked hard to keep AVEdge SE producing great streaming content. The lastest 
version is v7.7.1, updated 10-Jul-2017. Many plugins have gone away, but we've added 
several new ones. Try out Genesis Reborn, Showbox Arize and Bob Unrestricted. 

To check your current version; inside AVEdge SE, go to SYSTEM, then scroll down to 
SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME. To get the latest update: First quit out of 
AVEdge SE, then open the App draw and click on AVEdge Updater SE. Click 'I Agree' 
and wait for the new version to download and install. Allow any Add-on updates to 
complete before streaming.
