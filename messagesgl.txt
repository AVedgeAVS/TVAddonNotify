AVEdge SE has a new update. The lastest version is v6.1.5, updated 6-Apr-2017.
- Fixed Kodi Version message.
- Added One242415 and Evolve add-ons to Movies section menu.

To check your current version; inside AVEdge SE, go to SYSTEM, then scroll down to 
SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME.

To get the latest update: First quit the AVEdge SE app, then open the App draw and 
click on the AVEdge Updater SE app. Click 'I Agree' and wait for the new version 
to download and install.
