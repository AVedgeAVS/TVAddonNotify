Hello from us at AVEdge. With all the changes to the plugins that work in the system, 
we've worked hard to keep AVEdge ANX producing great streaming content. The lastest 
version is v4.5.0, updated 13-Jul-2017. Many plugins have gone away, but we've added 
several new ones. Try out Genesis Reborn, Showbox Arize and Bob Unrestricted. 

To check your current version; inside AVEdge ANX, go to SYSTEM, then scroll down to 
SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME. To get the latest update: First quit out of 
AVEdge ANX, then open the App draw and click on AVEdge Updater. Click 'Continue' 
and select AVEdge ANX Build wait for the new version to download and install. Allow 
any Add-on updates to complete before streaming.
