Hello again from your friends at AVEdge.

We've been hard at work making AVEdge ANX even better. We now have hundreds of Live TV channels available. To enable the Live channels:
1) Click on SETTINGS on the Main menu. 
2) Go down to LIVE TV and click on it. This brings up the LIVE TV menu.
3) Click the radio button on the top right that says ENABLE.
4) Wait about 3 minutes to load all the channels and program guides.
That's it. Now go back to the Main menu and click on LIVE TV.

This new version of AVEdge ANX also fixes the missing SPORTS menu item on the Main menu.

Latest update is 11-Nov-2016. Latest version is (v2.5.2)
To install the newest version of AVEdge ANX:
1) From your Apps screen, click on "AV Edge Updater".
2) Click on "Continue".
3) Select the build "AVEdge ANX Build vn.n.n". Now click "Yes" to update.

Thank you for choosing AVEdge and the AVEdge ANX Build. Happy streaming.
