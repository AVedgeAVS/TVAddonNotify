Latest update is 02-Aug-2017, version v5.7.0. 

Here's the latest update TO AVEdge HUB BUILD. We've made lots of changes to the 
sub-menus. There's new add-ons like Maverick and Halow to try out. Enjoy! 

To check your current version; inside AVEdge HUB, go to SYSTEM, then scroll down to 
SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME. To get the latest update: First quit out of 
AVEdge HUB, then open the App draw and click on AVEdge Updater. Click 'Continue' 
and select AVEdge HUB Build wait for the new version to download and install. Allow 
any Add-on updates to complete before streaming.
