Hello from AVEdge. Here's the latest update. It includes Arawak TV, Zen and another 
new movies and TV shows add-on Elysium. The lastest version is v5.6.1,

SERVICES and check the DEVICE NAME.

click on the AVEdge Updater app. Click 'Continue', then choose AVEdge HUB Build, click
